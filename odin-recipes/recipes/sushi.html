Description

Taco is a mexican dish that is very common in Sweden.

It's a vegan friendly dish u can replace the meat with beans for example.

Ingredients

  • Meat with rice
  • Taco chips
  • Guacamole

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Put the meat in pan to fry
  3. Prepare a salad while meat is frying
